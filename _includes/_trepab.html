• Haynes AM, Giacani L, Mayans MV, Ubals M, Nieto C, Pérez-Mañá C, et al. Efficacy of linezolid on <em>Treponema pallidum,</em> the syphilis agent: A preclinical study. EBioMedicine. 2021 Mar;65. [ DOI | ePrint ]

    Penicillin G, the current standard treatment for syphilis, has important drawbacks, but virtually no preclinical or clinical studies have been performed to identify viable alternatives. We tested, both <em>in vitro</em> and <em>in vivo</em>, three marketed antibiotics with adequate pharmacological properties to treat syphilis.
